COMPUTER ENGINEER

Computer engineer is a professional who specializes in the design, development, and maintenance of computer hardware and software systems.
They possess a deep understanding of computer architecture, digital electronics, programming languages, and algorithms. Computer engineers often
work on a wide range of projects, including designing integrated circuits, developing operating systems, creating networking systems, and optimizing
software performance. They play a crucial role in advancing technology by innovating new computing solutions and improving existing systems to meet the
evolving demands of various industries.
